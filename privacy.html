Privacy Policy

Extension Functionality

This extension allows users to display the current time for multiple selected cities and time zones directly in the browser. It is designed for quick and convenient world time viewing.

Use of Storage Permission

The extension uses the browser's storage permission solely to save your custom city list and active timezone settings locally in your browser. No data is uploaded or synchronized to any external server.

Data Usage Commitment

All data stored by this extension is used exclusively for displaying city and timezone information within the extension. We do not collect, analyze, sell, or use your data for any purpose other than the intended functionality of the extension.

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact the developer.
